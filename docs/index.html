Linkora

AI-powered chat assistant for finding local service providers

Linkora lets you describe what you need in plain language. The AI assistant Elin asks a few clarifying questions, then searches for matching local providers via Google Maps, ranks results by relevance, and generates a ready-to-send contact email on your behalf — all through a simple chat interface.

Linkora is fully open source under the MIT License. Source code: github.com/Fides-Connect/linkora
